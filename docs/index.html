Pages for Cat Curious

An App to collect and vote cates.

In short you can browse cats voted on by users in the collection middle tab. in the left tab you can vote cats in a silly game. Pet Adopt Talk in a KMK/MKK format. on the third tab you can dispatch a notification to receive a cat spirit on the next day, and decide to adopt them by saving their information locally on your device.
