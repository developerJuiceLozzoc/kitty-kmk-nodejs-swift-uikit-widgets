Pages for Cat Curious

An App to collect and vote cates.

In short you can collect curated community cats via the app on the App/Google Play store, and you can also browze and vote cats on the company website (https://developerjuice.xyz/apps).
The web application and native app has 3 tabs and experiences at the bottom of the view port. in the left tab you can vote cats in a silly game. Pet Adopt Talk in a KMK/MKK format. on the third tab you can dispatch a notification to receive a cat spirit on the next day, and decide to adopt them by saving their information locally on your device.
